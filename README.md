## 🧠 Core Trading Strategies

To succeed in prediction markets, you need a robust, mathematical approach. Our bot comes pre-configured with three foundational trading principles designed to protect capital and generate consistent yield:

### 1. Risk-Free Arbitrage ⚖️
In prediction markets, the combined price of "Yes" and "No" shares should theoretically equal $1.00. However, during high volatility, the combined price often drops below $1.00. The bot constantly scans the order book to buy both sides of a market when this anomaly occurs, locking in a mathematically guaranteed profit regardless of the outcome.

### 2. Directional Trading with Hedging 📉📈
When a clear trend emerges in the underlying asset (e.g., a sudden Bitcoin breakout), the bot engages in directional trading. It places bets on the statistically more likely outcome while simultaneously hedging on the opposite side. This sophisticated hedging mechanism minimizes downside exposure and smooths out your equity curve.

### 3. Strict Risk Management 🛡️
Capital preservation is the secret to long-term algorithmic trading success. The algorithm features automatic locking of both profits and losses. By setting strict stop-loss and take-profit parameters, the bot ensures that a sudden market reversal doesn't wipe out your daily gains.
